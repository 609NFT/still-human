About the Series

Still Human is an independent conversation series grounded in a simple idea: technology starts with people.

This is not a show about tools or hype. It's a space for thoughtful conversations about how AI is reshaping learning, work, leadership, and identity — and what remains distinctly human as that change accelerates.

Each episode brings together educators, technologists, and industry leaders to reflect on what's changing, what's uncertain, and how we navigate it.

The views expressed are those of the participants and do not represent any single institution.

Episode 01

What Does It Mean to Be Still Human?

The series opener. A wide-ranging conversation about technology, people, and the accelerating impact of AI on learning, work, and identity. The most important work in the age of AI is not technical obedience — it's cultivating self-understanding, ethical awareness, and the capacity to steer technology with purpose.

Episode 02

Leading Through Uncertainty as AI Accelerates

With Andy Deninger, CTO at IPS Group. Leadership becomes less about certainty and more about navigating complexity — asking better questions, embracing ambiguity, and creating environments where teams can learn, iterate, and evolve. Curiosity, humility, trust, and perspective are not diminished by AI. They're made more essential.
